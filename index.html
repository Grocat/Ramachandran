About

L Ramachandran

L Ramachandran was born in 1979 in India. Self-taught photographer who has huge passion towards photography and arts. He started his career as an assistant photographer in an advertisement agency in Chennai. With that experience he started a new advertisement agency named THRANSIA in the year 2004 focusing mainly on creative photography. His creative photographs scored various awards for photography and exhibited his works in Famous art galleries all over the world. Earlier he started doing Photography for commercial purpose. Once he found his deep passion and connection towards nature and its beauty he decided to explore capturing beautiful form of women along with nature. He went abroad for photo shoot to work with international magazines. His passion and hard work let him enter into the highest selling magazine in the world market "PLAYBOY".
